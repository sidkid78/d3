NAR Settlement Compliance & Post-Settlement Infrastructure

This documentation outlines the structural alignment of Dwellingly’s commission infrastructure with the National Association of Realtors (NAR) proposed settlement terms, ensuring all buyer representation agreements are legally binding and transparent.

1.0 Mandatory Written Agreements

In accordance with Section 2, Article IV of the settlement agreement, MLS participants working with a buyer must enter into a written agreement prior to touring a home. Dwellingly's BuyerLink™ protocol ensures that no digital tour request can be finalized without a counter-signed representation document.

2.0 Commission Transparency

Dwellingly infrastructure removes the "Commission" field from public-facing IDX feeds while maintaining a secure, authenticated back-channel for commission guarantee certificates. This ensures compliance with the rule prohibiting offers of compensation on the MLS while protecting the agent's right to earn.
